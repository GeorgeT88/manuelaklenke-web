# Product Brief: my-bmad-project

## Executive Summary

Ana is a seasoned professional translator with 20 years of experience specializing in German, Romanian, and English translations across legal, medical, technical, and literary domains. Despite her extensive expertise and strong word-of-mouth reputation, Ana currently has no online presence, limiting her ability to grow her client base beyond personal referrals. This project delivers a clean, professional single-page portfolio website built with React and Material UI that establishes Ana's digital footprint, showcases her credentials, and provides a direct channel for new client inquiries.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Ana is an experienced translator with a proven track record, but she has zero online discoverability. Potential clients searching for a German-Romanian-English translator cannot find her, and referrals have no way to verify her credibility before making contact.

### Problem Impact

Without an online presence, Ana's growth is capped by the reach of her personal network. She is invisible to clients who search online for translation services, and she loses opportunities to competitors who have established websites. Word-of-mouth alone cannot scale.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Ana does not use freelance platforms (ProZ, Fiverr, etc.), preferring to operate independently. These platforms also dilute a translator's brand, impose fees, and don't allow full control over how services and credentials are presented. There is no existing solution in place -- Ana has no website or digital presence of any kind.

### Proposed Solution

A professional single-page portfolio website built with React and Material UI featuring:
- **Hero section** with a clear professional tagline: "Professional Translation Services -- German | Romanian | English"
- **About section** highlighting Ana's 20 years of experience and professional story
- **Services section** showcasing four specialization areas: Legal, Medical, Technical, and Literary translation
- **Testimonials section** with three client testimonials for social proof
- **Contact section** with a form for direct client inquiries
- Smooth scroll anchor navigation, responsive design, and subtle flag-color accents representing her three working languages

### Key Differentiators

- **20 years of professional experience** across multiple translation domains
- **Rare language combination** -- German, Romanian, and English trilingual capability
- **Breadth of specialization** -- legal, medical, technical, and literary translation under one roof
- **Established reputation** -- existing testimonials and word-of-mouth credibility now made visible online

---

## Target Users

### Primary Users

**1. Legal Professionals (Lawyers & Law Firms)**
Lawyers and law firms requiring certified translation of contracts, legal agreements, court documents, and other legal materials between German, Romanian, and English. They prioritize accuracy, domain expertise, and professional reliability. A single mistranslation can have legal consequences, so they seek translators with proven experience in legal terminology.

**2. Publishers & Authors**
Publishing houses and independent authors seeking literary and book translations. They value a translator who can preserve voice, tone, and cultural nuance across languages. These clients often form long-term working relationships with translators they trust.

**3. Individuals**
People needing personal document translations -- birth certificates, diplomas, marriage certificates, immigration papers -- typically for cross-border administrative purposes between Germany, Romania, and English-speaking countries. They need reliable, certified translations and value clear communication and straightforward pricing.

### Secondary Users

N/A -- All three segments are direct users of Ana's translation services and interact with the website in the same way.

### User Journey

1. **Discovery** -- Potential client receives a word-of-mouth referral or searches online for a German-Romanian-English translator
2. **First Impression** -- They land on Ana's website and are greeted by a warm, approachable design with Ana's professional photo, her name, and the tagline *"Professional Translation Services -- German | Romanian | English."* The clean layout and warm color scheme immediately convey both competence and approachability
3. **Credibility Check** -- They scroll through her About section (20 years of experience), Services (matching their specific need), and Testimonials (social proof)
4. **Contact** -- Convinced of her expertise, they fill out the contact form or click the hero call-to-action button to request a quote
5. **Success Moment** -- They receive a prompt, professional response from Ana, confirming the website delivered on its promise of accessibility and credibility

---

## Success Metrics

The website's primary purpose is to serve as a professional digital presence that supports and validates word-of-mouth referrals. Success is not measured by traffic volume or conversion rates, but by the quality of the professional impression it creates.

**User Success Criteria:**
- A referred client visits the site and immediately understands what Ana offers, her experience, and how to contact her
- The site instills enough confidence that the visitor follows through on the referral and makes contact
- All three user segments (legal professionals, publishers/authors, individuals) can quickly identify that Ana serves their specific needs

**Website Success Criteria:**
- The site presents a warm, approachable, and professional image consistent with Ana's 20 years of experience
- All sections (Hero, About, Services, Testimonials, Contact) load correctly and display well on both desktop and mobile
- The contact form functions reliably and delivers inquiries to Ana

### Business Objectives

- Establish Ana's first professional online presence
- Provide a shareable link that Ana can give to referral sources and potential clients
- Reinforce Ana's credibility and professionalism to support word-of-mouth growth

### Key Performance Indicators

- Contact form is functional and delivers messages successfully
- Site is responsive and accessible across devices
- All content accurately represents Ana's services, experience, and professional brand
- Site loads quickly and presents a polished first impression

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

1. **Hero Section** -- Ana's name, professional photo, tagline (*"Professional Translation Services -- German | Romanian | English"*), and a call-to-action button
2. **About Section** -- Ana's professional story highlighting 20 years of experience across multiple translation domains
3. **Services Section** -- Four service cards: Legal, Medical, Technical, and Literary translation, each with a brief description
4. **Testimonials Section** -- Three client testimonials providing social proof and credibility
5. **Contact Section** -- Functional contact form (integrated via Formspree or EmailJS) for direct client inquiries
6. **Sticky Navbar** -- Fixed navigation bar with smooth scroll anchor links to each section
7. **Responsive Design** -- Mobile-first layout that works across all devices
8. **Warm, Approachable Design** -- Custom MUI theme with a warm color palette conveying professionalism and approachability
9. **Single-Page Architecture** -- All content on one scrollable page, no routing required

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Multilingual site versions (German, Romanian)
- Analytics or visitor tracking
- Blog or news section
- Client portal or file upload functionality
- Advanced SEO optimization
- Backend server or database
- Content management system (CMS)
- Online payment or quoting system

### MVP Success Criteria

- The site is live and accessible via a shareable URL
- All five sections render correctly on desktop and mobile
- The contact form successfully delivers messages to Ana
- The design conveys warmth, professionalism, and credibility
- A visitor can understand Ana's services, experience, and how to contact her within a single scroll

### Future Vision

- **Multilingual support** -- German and Romanian versions of the site to directly demonstrate Ana's language capabilities
- **SEO optimization** -- Improve discoverability for clients searching online for translation services
- **Blog/Resources section** -- Articles on translation topics to establish thought leadership
- **Client testimonials expansion** -- Add more testimonials over time as Ana's online client base grows
- **Portfolio samples** -- Showcase anonymized translation samples or case studies by domain
